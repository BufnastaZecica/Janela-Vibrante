Janela Vibrante is a 100% Portuguese company, specialised and experienced in the financial area.

We provide a quality and reliable service in the area of identification and negotiation of the best credit conditions for private customers.

We always treat our clients needs with professionalism, availability and trust. Our objective is to negotiate the best solutions with the main Banking Institutions for our customers. Our values stand for transparency, honesty and professionalism, which makes Janela Vibrante your ideal financial partner.

About

I'm João Paulo Ferreira, and my basic training is in the area of Management and Marketing. I worked at Banking for 30 years, specializing in the areas of credit management and consulting.

In 2021, I decided to move forward with Janela Vibrante, a credit intermediation project, with the aim of helping all families to negotiate their credit products.

At Janela Vibrante I have the possibility to apply all my technical knowledge and make the most of my 30 years of professional experience in leading teams and communication with the consumer.

Today I work not with one, but with the main banking/credit institutions, which allows me to negotiate and obtain the best housing or personal credit solution that meets the different needs of customers.

The presentation of the best proposal to the client is 100% free. How?

The choice will always be made by the customer, who will freely choose one of the presented proposals, with no impact on the credit conditions arising from our intermediation. Our remuneration comes from financial institutions.
